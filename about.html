Welcome

The world as a global village is an emphatic declaration of how close we are, yet separated by distance. We are a network of community of versatility in culture, believes, and aspirations. Our individual and communal survival depends entirely on our relationship and allegiance with other community of vast continents of people, ideas, and support systems. No matter the distance, help always come to us from our allies.

Digitalgene corporative society is an ally to trust and bank on. We are a community of network of people with the sole aim to support, provide hitch-free access to instant loans to initiate, start, and sustain business(es).

Digitalgene corporative society is a platform that connects you to your dream and future which begins with your responsive action through SAVINGS, LOANS, and INVESTMENT.

YOUR JOURNEY HERE

1. Indicate interest
2. Get an inducted member to recomend you
3. Sign up by reading our operations
4. Meet up the requirements
5. On acceptance, proceed by paying your registration fee (2000) naira.
6. Wait to be inducted into the society after we have checked and confirm your eligibility status.
7. You will receive a welcome notice into the society.
8. You would be advised to start saving a regular amount every month.
9. You are to pay straight to the bank that the corporative has chosen from where ever you are.
10. Keep your payment slip or online payment history from the bank.
11. You can ask for your corporative payment history from the account department of the society either to be sent to you online or hardcopy by requesting for it.
12. Members can apply for loans after 6 months of consistent savings.
13. You would be required to provide 2 members from the corporative to sign your loan request form.(Nothing more than that)
14. The corporative account department accepts the loan request form, work and screens the eligibility of it.
15. Loan is granted within 2 -3 working days having certified every due process as accredited by the society.

MEMBERSHIP REQUIREMENTS

All intended members must have and present the underlisted requirements for proper documentations before they are inducted into the society. 1. BVN (Compulsory )
2. Account Number (Compulsory )
And a clear front page of any one of the following: 3. NIN
4. Voter's Card
5. Driver's License
6. International Passport
7. Name and ID number of the member that recommended you to the SOCIETY.
