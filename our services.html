Our Services

Savings

All members are fit to begin their savings immediately they are inducted into the society. The savings procedures include;
1. Members are to save a particular amount monthly.
2. In the case that a member wants to increase or decrease their savings, they must write to the society for proper readjustment.
3. Irregularities of savings is not allowed to avoid members pushing in more funds within their first six (6) months of savings with the so aim of acquiring higher loans.

Loans

1. Having satisfied the regular SAVINGS of atleast six (6) months and passing the eligibility check, you can apply and have access to loans of lower interest rate compared to the commercial banks and no collateral except for 2 members required to sign as a surety for you.
2. You now have funds to invest into personal business(es), dreams and goals as you work to attaining life independency and financial freedom.
3. You have a simple and flexible repayment life style spread across 1 yr or one and half yr period based on your request of time.
4. You can apply for more loans once you are done repaying the formal.
5. You can increase your savings to apply for higher loans.

Investment

Members having met the eligibility check to obtain loans can invest in their choices of business(es) to maximise profit and attain self financial freedom. However, any investment(s) that would be undertaken by the corporative society would be duely communicated to all corporators of the society as this may require attestation and risk bearing undertaken by the interested members of the society. No investment(s) would be done on behave of the corporative without due consultation and approval by the members.
